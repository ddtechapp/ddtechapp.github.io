Why should I build something?

在HackNews上看到一句话，如果感到抑郁的话，做点什么？

老婆说我每个月都会感到抑郁，都会有一种是不是的陷入一种无法自拔的不爽之中，的确如此。随是如此，我不是那种感怀伤时的人。只是喜欢慨叹一些事情。

So, I should build something for the furture. I don’t need someone to know me. Because nobody can help me to fight.

One man with the laptop

write code day after day

someday, the man grows up

the company will come out

my dream is family dream

my wife needs me

my son needs me

I’m the hero…
